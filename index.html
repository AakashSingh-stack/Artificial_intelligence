Artificial intelligence

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the intelligence of machines or software, as opposed to the intelligence of human beings or animals. AI applications include advanced web search engines (e.g., Google Search), recommendation systems (used by YouTube, Amazon, and Netflix), understanding human speech (such as Siri and Alexa), self-driving cars (e.g., Waymo), generative or creative tools (ChatGPT and AI art), and competing at the highest level in strategic games

ChatGPT

ChatGPT (Chat Generative Pre-Trained Transformer) is a large language model-based chatbot developed by OpenAI and launched on November 30, 2022, notable for enabling users to refine and steer a conversation towards a desired length, format, style, level of detail, and language used. Successive prompts and replies, known as prompt engineering, are taken into account at each stage of the conversation as a context.[2] ChatGPT is built upon GPT-3.5 and GPT-4, from OpenAI's proprietary series of foundational GPT models, fine-tuned for conversational applications using a combination of supervised and reinforcement learning techniques.[3] ChatGPT was released as a freely available research preview, but due to its popularity, OpenAI now operates the service on a freemium model. It allows users on its free tier to access the GPT-3.5 based version, while the more advanced GPT-4 based version, as well as priority access to newer features, are provided to paid subscribers under the commercial name "ChatGPT Plus".

Lumbalabs

Luma AI is an innovative app and service developed by Luma Labs, a California-based company located in Palo Alto. It uses Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) to capture 3D images with stunning photo-realistic detail. This technology is similar to what high-end gaming uses to create graphics that are almost indistinguishable from reality through ray-tracing. Whether you're an AI development expert or just curious about the technology, Luma's NERF technology allows you to create beautiful 3D images and videos easily and quickly. The user interface is simple to navigate, making it easy to get started and explore the powerful features of this impressive software.
